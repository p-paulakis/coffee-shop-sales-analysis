# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of one week of coffee shop sales data to identify revenue trends and provide business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- **R** (tidyverse, sqldf, ggplot2)
- **SQL** for data querying

## Questions Answered
1. What is the total weekly revenue?
2. Which product is the best seller?
3. How does revenue vary by day of week?
4. Which products have the highest customer ratings?
5. What are Friday-specific sales patterns?
